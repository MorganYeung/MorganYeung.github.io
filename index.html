I am an Embedded Software Developer at HelpWear.

I am an embedded engineer interested in solving complex multidisciplinary problems. At Helpwear, I work on firmware, testing as well as R&D for the Heartwatch, an wearable device aiming to become the next gold-standard device for ECG data collection.

Prior to this, I had completed a total of 12 months work with Formlabs and there I worked in hardware systems manufacturing, designing testers and jigs for 3D printers. I also programmed the calibration routines for the Form3 Printer to ensure high quality prints. Before that, I completed two 4 month work terms with Flex (previously known as Flextronics), designing testers for automotive modules.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy rock climbing, working out and playing ultimate frisbee intramurals. I am also interested in photography.
